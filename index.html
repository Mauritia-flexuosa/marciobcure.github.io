About Me

My name is Marcio Baldissera Cure, and I am a Brazilian researcher with a Master's and PhD in Ecology. I have extensive experience in field research and data analysis. My passion lies in understanding and preserving natural and human systems from a holistic perspective, utilizing advanced methodologies and innovative technologies. My undergraduate degree in Environmental Management from the Federal University of Pampa (Unipampa) provides me with an interdisciplinary approach to problem-solving and enables me to think about transdisciplinary strategies.

Portfolio
